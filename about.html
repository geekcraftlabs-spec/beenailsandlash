About Bee Nails and Lash Studio

Where beauty meets calm precision in the heart of Pretoria Central

Our Story

With over 10 years of passionate expertise, Bee Nails and Lash Studio has become Pretoria Central's go-to haven for flawless lash extensions, creative nail artistry, and stunning hair braiding styles.

We specialize in premium techniques — from classic to volume lashes, acrylic/polygel/gum nail sets, and intricate protective braiding — always delivered with meticulous attention to detail and a personal touch. Our mission is simple: help every client feel confident and radiant in a serene, welcoming space that feels like a peaceful escape.

Conveniently located at Central House, Pretoria Central, our boutique offers easy access and a calm atmosphere perfect for your beauty ritual.

What We Specialize In

Lash Extensions

Classic, volume, hybrid — customized to enhance your natural beauty with precision and care.

Nail Artistry

Acrylic, polygel, fiber nails, and unique designs — over 10 years of expert craftsmanship.

Hair Braiding

Beautiful protective styles including box braids, cornrows, twists & more (no hair installations yet).
